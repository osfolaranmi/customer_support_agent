Title: Northstar CRM Email Sync Guide
Last updated: 2026-07-08
Owner: Email Integrations Support

Northstar CRM email sync helps sales teams see customer email activity on CRM timelines. Email sync is available on Pro and Business plans.

Supported providers:

- Google Workspace Gmail.
- Microsoft 365 Outlook.

What sync can do:

- Log messages sent to or received from CRM contacts.
- Add email activity to contact, company, and deal timelines.
- Create new contacts from email recipients when enabled.
- Respect excluded domains and private email rules.
- Show sync status in Settings > Integrations.

What sync does not do:

- It does not send bulk marketing campaigns.
- It does not guarantee that every historical email will sync.
- It does not sync emails from unsupported providers.
- It does not bypass Google or Microsoft admin restrictions.
- It does not expose emails to users who do not have permission to view the related CRM record.

Setup checklist:

1. Confirm the workspace is on Pro or Business.
2. Confirm the user has permission to connect email.
3. Go to Settings > Integrations.
4. Select Google Workspace or Microsoft 365.
5. Connect the account.
6. Approve provider permissions.
7. Choose whether to sync existing contacts only or create new contacts.
8. Add excluded domains such as personal email domains or internal-only domains.
9. Save sync settings.
10. Wait for initial sync to complete.

Recommended private email exclusions:

- Personal domains used by employees.
- Legal or HR domains.
- Internal-only aliases.
- Vendor addresses that should not appear in sales timelines.

Troubleshooting missing emails:

1. Confirm the contact email matches the email message exactly.
2. Confirm the message is not excluded by domain rules.
3. Confirm the user is connected in Settings > Integrations.
4. Wait up to 30 minutes after initial setup.
5. Reconnect the provider if sync status shows disconnected.
6. Ask the Google or Microsoft admin whether third-party app access is blocked.

Troubleshooting duplicate contacts from email sync:

1. Check whether contact auto-creation is enabled.
2. Review Contacts > Duplicates.
3. Confirm the same person does not use multiple email aliases.
4. Merge duplicates manually after reviewing suggested matches.
5. Disable automatic contact creation if the team prefers manual review.

Security and privacy:

- The assistant must not ask the customer to forward private email content.
- The assistant should not ask for OAuth tokens or credentials.
- If private messages appear on CRM timelines unexpectedly, escalate to Security Response or Technical Support depending on severity.
- If email sync belongs to a former employee, an Admin should disconnect it immediately and review access.

Escalation triggers:

- Private messages synced unexpectedly.
- Email sync fails for all users.
- OAuth app approval fails for the whole workspace.
- Former employee mailbox remains connected.
- Customer reports missing sales-critical messages before a live event.
